AiBlocks is an open network of digital money and applications

AiBlocks make it possible for you to convert any asset class into a digital representation. Gold, silver, properties, fiat currencies and many more. We design it in a way that all asset classes can be exchanged easily and quickly, anytime, anywhere.

Secure

Utilizing blockchain technology to update and verify all transactions.

Powerful

Flexibility to utilise tokens to represent any physical asset.

Quick

Lightning speed confirmations with the ability to process thousands of transactions per second.

AiBlocks transforms money

Equipped with the latest technology, to help you transform physical assets to digital representation without much knowledge on coding. This gives you the power to digitise your banking system with a lower entry barrier.

Assets exchange

Exchange assets seamlessly without hassle all on a single platform. Built in the app is everything you need to swap for tokens.

Ai based consensus protocol

Leverage on tokens that are currently on the AiBlocks chain.

Micropayment services

You can use AiBlocks to pay with minimal transaction fees using Delos, with almost instant confirmations.

Developer friendly tools

You can obtain all information about how to get started quickly.
